MYTHS:

  1. COVID-19 does not affect children.

    2. FALSE

    This claim is false. Even though children haven't died often and even though they don't show symptoms, they can still be carriers and get older people sick. Also it will make children's body weaker.

  2. If you wash your hands you will not get the Coronavirus.

    3. PARTLY TRUE

    This claim is partly true. According to WHO washing your hand for 20 seconds with soap will help you immensely when trying to avoid the virus. But, you still have to take showers and just washing your hands won't stop the virus but it helps.


  3. The Coronavirus makes you lung weaker.

    4. TRUE

    This claim is unfortunately true. The Coronavirus does many things to your lungs, but ultimatley it will weaken your lungs. Do get more information, check out USA TODAY.

  4. Masks can portect me from COVID-19.

    5. FALSE

    This claim is false. Even though many people have been seen wearing masks and even though they are at a popular demand, they won't help us not get the coronavirus. Masks are oly used for people who have the coronavirus so it doesn't spread to other people.

  5. COVID-19 is man made.

    6. TRUE

    This claim is false. It seems it jumped from an animal in a WET market in Wuhan, China. But it has not been proven beyond doubt. It is also said that China was creating a bioweapon and this was leaked. China also accuses the US of planting the virus in China. The truth is far from known, but it most likely seems like it got passed to humans from animals.

  6. Drinking lots of water can flush out the virus infection.

    7. PARTLY TRUE

    Always good to be hydrated generally. But, drinking water will not help prevent or cure the COVID infection.
